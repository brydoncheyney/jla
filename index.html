Breathe limited edition EP release - 2012/10/01

je$us loves amerika follow up last year's FYA EP with the release of the Breathe EP - a SEVEN TRACK EP of new material and remixes, by friends Concrete Lung, Danny Carnage and i!, as well as the band themselves. The lead track is once more produced by Phil Barry of Cubanate and Be My Enemy fame and the EP is available in a very limited run of 100 hand numbered cd copies, on sale at the Armalyte Industries online store.

Alternatively, you can pick up an early copy in London when the band play with Sulpher, Jayce Lewis and The Officers later this month at the Camden Underworld - tshirt bundles will again be on offer at the gig and any remaining stock will appear for sale online after the event...
